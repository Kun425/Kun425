Research Field

Understanding the ion diffusion within solid-state electrolytes and at interfaces.

Responsibilities

  • Responsible for the fundamental research on solid-state electrolytes and solid-state batteries.

Job Requirements

  • Candidates must hold a Ph.D. degree in materials science, physics, chemistry, or a related field.

  • Candidates with experience in solid electrolyte synthesis will be preferentially considered.
  • Exhibit excellent teamwork, communication skills in either English or Chinese.
