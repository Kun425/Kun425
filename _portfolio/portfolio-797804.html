Research Field

Cathodes for lithium-ion or sodium-ion batteries.

Responsibilities

  • Responsible for conducting in-depth research on energy materials and devices using synchrotron X-ray or neutron characterization techniques.
  • Participate in experiments, discuss and propose experimental plans with the Principal Investigator (PI), and conduct experiments at synchrotron sources or neutron sources with group members.

Job Requirements

  • Candidates must possess Ph.D. degree in materials science, physics or related fields, with prior experiment experience using synchrotron X-ray or neutrons.
  • A certain level of understanding on energy storage materials.
  • Exhibit excellent teamwork, communication skills in either English or Chinese.
  • Good English writing skills are essential, with the ability to compose manuscripts, research reports, and prepare conference materials.
