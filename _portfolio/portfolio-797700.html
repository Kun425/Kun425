Responsibilities

  • Conduct theoretical calculations using the advanced computational platforms and supercomputers.
  • Collaborate closely with experimental teams to enhance comprehension of experimental findings.
  • Assist the Principal Investigators (PIs) in developing high-throughput computational methods integrated with artificial intelligence.
  • Compose manuscripts, reports, and conference materials.

Job Requirements

  • Applicants must possess a Ph.D. degree in physics, chemistry, materials science, computational science, or a related field.
  • Candidates should have a profound understanding of computational principles and tools, with the ability to independently carry out research.
  • Exhibit excellent teamwork, communication skills in either English or Chinese, and active participation in academic collaborations.
  • Good English writing skills are essential, with the ability to compose manuscripts, research reports, and prepare conference materials.
  • Applicants with experience in any of the following areas will be preferred: first-principles calculations, molecular dynamics simulations, finite element analysis, multi-physics simulations, or other related computational methods
